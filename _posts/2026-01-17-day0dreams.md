### The "Home Server" Experiment

I originally started this website on my own [Home server](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Home_server) . While it was a great learning experience, the maintenance became a hurdle I couldn't ignore. 

Managing a server becomes incredibly tedious when you face:
1.  **Limited Physical Access:** Hard to fix hardware issues when you're away for long stretches.
2.  **Power Constraints:** No electricity backup makes uptime a constant battle.

> A friend suggested I try github pages, and honestly, the simplicity is refreshing. It allows me to focus on writing rather than sysadmin tasks.
